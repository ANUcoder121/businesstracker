Who we are?

We are on mission to extend the benefits of technology for everyone to use it and get most out of it for betterment of everyone’s business.

Business Tracker is groundbreaking innovative App solution for Indian any type of Small and Medium Scale business which simplifies the Accounting and Billing system with layman language without any hiccups for Credit/Debit and old Black Window. It has beautiful screens through which in easy business language you can create Sale Invoice, Estimation & Quotation, Delivery Challan, Purchase Orders, Track real time Inventory, Simplified CRM and many more features.

We want every small/ medium scale business should go on digitalize as per time and get benefit of technology to more focus on their own core business but not on accounting entries so they can rely on Business Tracker app to maintain their accounting.
